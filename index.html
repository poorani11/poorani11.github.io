Design Methodology

My methodology involves the process of solving problems through the use of
design modes like thought, expression and production.

Thought

I like to have a holistic approach to problem solving. Through the use of experience, research and comparative data search.

Expression

I then dissect & analyze each part of the problem, working with schematic diagrams, design principles and aesthetics.

Production

Involves creating and articulating between the visual and functional parts of the problem and make them coherent.
